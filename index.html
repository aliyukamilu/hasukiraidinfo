HasukiRaid Hunt Guide

To order raids on Hasuki Raid:

  1. Go to the website and click on the button " Publish a hunt"
  2. Connect your crypto wallet, different wallet options will be available
  3. Choose between Raid 2 Earn and Tweet2Earn:
    1. Raid2Earn - Orders Hasuki raiders to boost engagement on your own created tweets.
      • Complete the form with necessary details: unique TweetID, characters you want in the comment, minimum amount of followers a raider account needs, and which words should be included in the comments on the tweet.
      • Choose the formula (50, 100, 180, or 380 raider requests) and the token you want to distribute for ordering those options ($SOL or $BOOP).
    2. Tweet2Earn - Orders Hasuki raiders to compose a tweet based on the provided information by yourself.
      • Provide the unique description of the task to produce the tweets, numbers of characters you want in the comment, and which words should be included in the tweet.
      • Choose the formula (75 or 150 raider requests) and the token you want to distribute for ordering those options ($SOL or $BOOP).
      • Recommendation: Go to the Hasuki Discord server and give guidelines through the Ticket Service to ensure the right information can be shared to make the tweets as good as possible.
  4. If you choose to use $BOOP but don't have the token in your wallet, you can swap it on the DEX of Raydium or Jupiter.
    • A liquidity pool for $BOOP and $SOL is available on the Solana blockchain.
    • The token address for $BOOP is CCRJohp9bfQdhZCvCe7MzUQuXMJkKBi4XCjUq3A2YqN8
    • The swap link for Raydium: Click here.
  5. Once you have completed all the necessary steps, you will see the formula you have chosen at the bottom of the screen.
  6. If everything is as you want it, click the " Submit Tweet" button, select the token you want to reward the raiders with and approve the transaction. Your tasks will be posted on the platform for the raiders to fulfill.
